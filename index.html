About Me

My name is Worawit Tepsan, and I am a lecturer at the International College of Digital Innovation (ICDI) at Chiang Mai University. I hold a PhD in Pure Mathematics and am currently applying my expertise in mathematics to the field of data analytics.

Teaching

Guided by my love for Pure Math, I delve into the world of DATA through teaching. My journey from a TA at the University of Houston to a faculty member at CMU has seen me cover including Calculus, Social Networks, Big Data, and Digital Tool.

Research Focus

My research bridges mathematics and computer science, focusing on Data Analytics, Machine Learning, and Applied Mathematics.
